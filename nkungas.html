About Our Family

The Nkunga M'Mugambi Kimba family traces its roots to the highlands of Kenya, where our ancestors established a strong community based on unity, hard work, and cultural preservation.

This platform serves as a digital archive of our family tree and a place to share news and updates through our blog. The family tree is interactive - you can click on any family member to see their descendants and use the + buttons to add new family members.

We welcome contributions from all family members to help grow this resource and keep our family connected across generations and geographical locations.
